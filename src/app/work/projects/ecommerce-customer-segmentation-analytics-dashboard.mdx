## Overview

An interactive data analytics dashboard built for e-commerce businesses to understand their sales patterns, track KPIs, and segment customers using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis.

## Key Features

- **Data Cleaning Pipeline**: Automated data preprocessing and cleaning for uploaded CSV datasets.
- **Sales Analysis & KPI Tracking**: Visual dashboards for revenue trends, top products, and key business metrics.
- **RFM Customer Segmentation**: Machine learning-based customer segmentation using Scikit-learn for targeted business insights.
- **Interactive Visualization**: Dynamic charts, filters, and downloadable reports with a modern responsive UI.

## Technologies Used

- **Python & Streamlit**: For rapid dashboard development with interactive widgets.
- **Pandas & NumPy**: For data manipulation, cleaning, and analysis.
- **Matplotlib & Seaborn**: For statistical visualizations and charts.
- **Scikit-learn**: For RFM-based customer segmentation and clustering.

## Challenges and Learnings

Handling messy real-world CSV data with missing values and inconsistent formats required building a robust data cleaning pipeline. Balancing interactivity with performance in Streamlit for larger datasets was another challenge that improved my understanding of data engineering.

## Outcome

A production-ready analytics dashboard that empowers e-commerce businesses to make data-driven decisions through visual insights and customer segmentation.
